About Me

I'm Kenneth James Mendoza, a web developer, digital artist, and 3D modeler based in Peoria, AZ. I specialize in creating interactive web experiences and developing applications that solve real-world problems.

My passion lies in building engaging and functional web applications, like the Divine Gems game you’ll find below. I hold a Bachelor of Arts in Media Arts and Animation from The Art Institute of Phoenix, and my professional experience includes an IMDb film credit and work in the industry.

In addition to my development work, I have 5 1/2 years of experience teaching in a public charter school system, and I am a licensed Empowerment Scholarship Account (ESA) vendor with the Arizona Department of Education. I am a highly qualified instructor with a Black Belt in Japanese Jiu-Jitsu (Sensei) and Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (Professor with Instructor Bars).
